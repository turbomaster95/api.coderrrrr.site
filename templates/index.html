webiDNS v0.0.1!

The available endpoints are:

- [GET] /domains
- [GET] /domain/{name}/{tld}
- [POST] /domain
- [PUT] /domain/{key}
- [DELETE] /domain/{key}
- [GET] /tlds

Ratelimits are as follows: 5 requests per 10 minutes on `[POST] /domain`.
